Deployment topologies

Choose the footprint that matches your privacy, latency, and cost profile. Airos tooling supports containerized rollouts across hardware tiers.

  • On-device: Wearables and mobile hardware with quantized models.
  • Edge cluster: Micro data centers for schools, offices, and research labs.
  • Hybrid cloud: Burst to GPUs for heavy workloads, keep context local.
