Quickstart

1. Clone the repository and explore the sample agents.
2. Provision dependencies: Docker, Node.js, Python, and your preferred GPU runtime.
3. Configure credentials for OpenAI, Anthropic, Qwen, or on-prem models.
4. Launch the voice-first demo client and experiment with local inference.
